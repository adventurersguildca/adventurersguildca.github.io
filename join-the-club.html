Join the club

Founded in Canada, the Adventurers Guild promotes adventure and exploration of land and sea, with a component of supporting others. Guild members have been on motorbike adventures in Canada, the US and Mexico, Indonesia, Africa, NZ and Australia, and sailing in the South Pacific, Pacific Northwest, Africa and the Caribbean.
