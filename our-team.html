Alex Hamilton

Alex, originally from NZ, has sailed in the South Pacific, raced in Africa with a Navy Captain, toured the Pacific Northwest and the Caribbean. After riding motorbikes for more than three decades, he’s still keen on more. Alex has instructed road techniques for motorcycles for 10 years for the BC Safety Council, and ridden dirt bikes in NZ, Canada, the US and Indonesia. One of his favourite road trips was New Orleans to Tennessee through Mississippi on the Natchez Trace.
